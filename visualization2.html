Visualization 2

Interpretation

Here I provided a legend to show you which circle is what day. There is no relation to the colors, just more random and a visual appeal. The larger the circle, the more incidents that happend on that day.

Discussion

Getting sizes and placement was very difficult in matching my tableu. You really need to think about x and y coordinate and where that point of reference is. I used the pack object which made all the circles in a pack that put them together and puth them at the center of the graph. It seemed to automatically generate the x and y and the radius which is what made it difficult. The goal here is to show the user the days of the week that would hold more crime. This shows the weekend is higher in crime which can help you to be more vigilent during those times.
